Bibimbap

Description

Warm white rice topped with namul or kimchi and gochujang, soy sauce, or doenjang

Ingredients

Vegetables & others

  • White Rice
  • 250g Spinach
  • 350g Bean Sprouts
  • 100g Shiitake Mushroom
  • 120g Carrot
  • 3 Eggs
  • Toasted Seaweed
  • Oil
  • Salt

Meat and Meat Sauce

  • 100g minced beef
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp minced garlic

Bibimbap Sauce

  • 2 Tbsp gochujang
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp water
  • 1 Tbsp roasted sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp minced garlic

Steps:

  1. Cook the desired amount of white rice
  2. For meat, mix the beef mince with the meat sauce listed above. Marinate the meat for about 30 mins while you are working on other ingredients to enhance the flavour. Add some cooking oil into a wok and cook the meat on medium high to high heat. It takes about 3 to 5 mins to thoroughly cook it.
  3. Mix the bibimbap sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  4. Cook spinach and bean sprouts.
  5. Rinse, peel and julienne the carrots. Add some cooking oil and 1/4 tsp of fine sea salt in a wok and cook the carrots on medium high to high heat for 2 to 3 mins.
  6. Clean, rinse, slice and lighly cook the shiitake mushrooms on medium high heat.
  7. Make fried eggs.
  8. Mix everything together and serve.
